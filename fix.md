No transaction search from the dashboard. The main dashboard only shows the 5 most recent transactions. There's no global search. Users with large histories have no way to find a specific entry without going to Transactions and manually filtering.

No budget alerts or notifications. There's no mechanism to warn users when they're approaching or have exceeded their budget mid-month. This is a core feature expectation for a finance tracker.

The recurring template system has no end date. Once created, a recurring entry generates transactions indefinitely. Users have no way to say "this subscription ended in March." Add an optional endDate field to recurring_templates.
